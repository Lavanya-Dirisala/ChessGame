Chess Game -

Initially Game starts with White Player 
here white player enters the Source Position (Piece Position) which he wants to move
and also enters Destination Position where he wants to move the Piece

Next Game Switches to Black Player 
again black player enters the Source Position (Piece Position) which he wants to move
and also enters Destination Position where he wants to move the Piece

In our game if white player checks Black King then White Player will Be announced as Winner
when any error will encounter GameStatus will be Error 
if WhiteWins -  GameStatus will be WhiteWin status 
BlackWins -  GameStatus will be BlackWin Status
